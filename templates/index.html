Available Endpoints:
GET /api/videos

List all tracked videos with pagination

Parameters: page (default: 1), per_page (default: 10)

POST /api/videos

Add a new video to track

Body: {"video_id": "YouTube_Video_ID"}

GET /api/videos/{video_id}/stats

Get statistics for a specific video

Parameters: start_date (optional), end_date (optional)

DELETE /api/videos/{video_id}

Stop tracking a video and delete its data
